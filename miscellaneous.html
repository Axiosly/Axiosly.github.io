Some other thing I want to say

I am enthusiastic, hard worker, and flexible to learn new things.

My dream is to become an indie game developer and a white hat when I was a young student in Ningbo High school. Therefore, I joined C language society and Arduino society in school. And I have taught myself computer art, video edit, 3D modeling, electronic music production during undergraduate period. Also, I have many other hobbies such as Launchpad (MIDI), photography, PCB design, earphone DIY, Cosplay, Hanfu, etc.

Of all that I have done in my undergraduate life, I am most proud to report the vulnerability of SQL and VB.NET Framework in college academic administration system which can be used to modify for instructors' account recording results. However, by chance coincidence, I chose the Strategic Planning of Microsoft HoloLen as my final year project. Since then, I lost my self in HCI.

I'm really appreciate the god arranged such a chance for me to join the XrVis Lab in XJTLU. In here, I meet my supervisors and schoolmates who are supporting me and fighting with me.

I believe everyone will be better!
